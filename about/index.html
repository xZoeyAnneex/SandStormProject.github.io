About

About the Group

SandStormProject isn't just one project, we split into multiple groups such as FrostByte Games. The SandStormProject's main goal is to re-invent gaming, hence the tagline "Re-Inventing Gaming for the Future". We try to change the way people think of games through modding, YouTube videos and creating games that we hope everyone will enjoy.
